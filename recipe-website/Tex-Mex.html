Tex-Mex Burger With Cajun Mayo

Description

An easy-to-follow recipe for a fiery and appetizing burger which mixes the Texan and Mexican style of burger to produce the best of both styles by utilizing Cajun mayo sauce to give the burger a spicy kick.

Ingredients (for 4 burgers)

  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 jalape√±o pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1/2 cup of diced white onion
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 slices of pepper jack cheese
  • 4 split hamburger buns
  • 4 leaves of lettuce
  • 4 slices of tomato

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise and 1 teaspoon of Cajun seasoning together. Set aside.

  2. Mix the ground sirloin, jalapeno pepper, onion, garlic, 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning, and Worcestershire sauce together in a large bowl using your hands. Divide the mixture into 4 balls, and flatten into patties.

  3. Lightly oil the Preheated grill surface, and place the patties on the grill. Cook for about 5 minutes per side, or until well done. During the last 2 minutes, lay a slice of cheese on top of each patty. Spread the seasoned mayonnaise onto the insides of the buns. Put burgers in the buns, and top with lettuce and tomato to serve.
